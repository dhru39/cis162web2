Roster

Sledge

Sledge is an Attacking Operator in Rainbow Six Siege. He excels at creating new lines of fire and entry points, harassing defenders with his tactical breaching hammer.

Hibana

Hibana is an Attacking Operator in Rainbow Six Siege. She is equipped with the X-KAIROS, a 40 mm caliber launcher that fires explosive pellets that can be detonated simultaneously from a distance, allowing her to deliver innovative assaults by creating lines of sights or new pathways.

Ace

Ace is an Attacking Operator in Rainbow Six Siege. He is equipped with the S.E.L.M.A. Aqua Breacher, which uses hydraulic pressure to burst through breakable and reinforced surfaces, allowing him to create new entry points or lines of sight.

Iana

Iana is an Attacking Operator in Rainbow Six Siege. She is equipped with the Gemini Replicator, a remotely-controlled holographic copy of herself that can move and produce sound, allowing her to collect information and fool her opponents.

Kaid

Kaid is a Defending Operator in Rainbow Six Siege. He is equipped with the “Rtila” Electroclaw, which can electrify reinforced walls and hatches, barbed wire, and deployable shields, making it tougher for the enemy to breach.

Vigil

Vigil is a Defending Operator in Rainbow Six Siege. He is equipped with the Electronic Rendering Cloak (ERC-7), which uses Diminished Reality technology to remove perceivable stimuli from its direct environment, allowing him to remain undetected.

Jager

Jäger is a Defender in Rainbow Six Siege. He can help his team create a defensive stronghold before roaming the map to intercept flanking attackers. He is capable of destroying incoming projectiles due to his unique deployable gadget: the Active Defense System or ADS.

Azami

Azami is a Defender in Rainbow Six Siege. She is armed with a modified kunai that releases a compound that solidifies on surfaces, allowing players to patch up walls and create brand new defensive lines.

Kapkan

Kapkan is a Defending Operator in Rainbow Six Siege. He is equipped with the Entry Denial Device (EDD-MK II), a packed C4 charge activated when motion is detected, which can be placed on door and window frames to deny key entry points for attackers.
